General


What is Rutogo.com?


Rutogo is a marketplace for all the cab operators in your city, be it radio taxi or outstation taxi. We integrate all the taxi operators under one platform so that you do not need to call multiple vendors for pricing, availability and type of car just visit our website or app and book the most economical cab.



Where we are operational?


Currently we operate out of Delhi-NCR. But we are expanding to your city soon, do check our website frequently and get updated with the information.



What are the advantages using Rutogo.com?

We are a one stop shop for all taxi needs. For local travel you can get the pricing and availability of major radio taxi operators like Ola, Uber, Meru and Taxiforsure. For intercity travel you can get the quotes of all the boutique cab operators, including the ones in your area so that we ensure you don't waste time in calling multiple operators.



Booking Related


How do I book a cab?


Fireup the app and choose your category


  • Local Travel:

Just enter your pickup and drop location and click on search. You get a list of all the radio operators in your city with pricing and availability, click on book now you will be directed to their app if installed else a mobile website will be opened in the same app where you can book the taxi. You can also connect to the customer care of the respective taxi operator and proceed to booking.


  • Outstation Travel:

Enter your pickup and drop location and click on search, you will get a list of all the vendors with rating availability and pricing you are free to book a cab from the vendor of your choice. Once you book the cab wait for your driver details and pay in cash once you are done with the trip.



Why should I book a cab from Rutogo.com?

Unlike any single vendor or cab provider, Rutogo.com offers cabs from multiple vendors with different prices, types of cab and even tailor-made packages. Our extensive network lets you book a cab and choose from multiple options according to your budget and preference.


What if I want to change or cancel the booking?

Please call us at 9999-088-077 to change or cancel your booking. In case of changing the booking, the pick-up address cannot be changed and booking will depend on availability of desired car type on a desired travel date. Any additional charges, if applicable, will be paid by the customer.


What is the minimum time to book a cab?

You need to book at least 3hours prior to the journey incase you need it before that time do call our customer care number at 9999-088-077

Payment related


How should I pay for my booking?


At present you need to pay in cash after you have made the journey. We are also coming up with an online payment option soon so you don't have to struggle with carrying the cash.



Are there any other charges?


Apart from the regular cab charges you need to pay the driver a DA per day as mentioned by the vendor and toll, parking and state taxes are applicable on actual



Do I get an invoice?


Yes, you will get the invoice after the completion of the trip to your registered email address.
